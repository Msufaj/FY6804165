Abstract

An aircraft electrical system is an independent network of components that generate, transfer, apply and store electrical energy. 
    All but the most basic aircraft designs require an electrical system as a crucial part to ensure safety, navigation and overall efficiency. 
    An aircraft powered by a single piston engine for general aviation and a modern multi-engine commercial jet have quite different electrical systems in terms of capacity and complexity. 
    That said, many of the fundamental parts of an aircraft's electrical system are similar across all detailed levels.
